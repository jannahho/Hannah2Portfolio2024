SweepEase

Overview

Design Challenge: How Might We make difficult and/or dreaded household cleaning chores easier and more enjoyable?

SweepEase is a solution to hard floor cleaning: Just one tool that will pick up all debris of different sizes. A non-electric mechanical tool with a moist disposable cloth on the bottom to wipe down your floors, but also with an overhead claw that scrapes in any larger debris.

Get the entire range of floor debris in one pass. The SweapEase is intuitive to use with itâ€™s claw controlled by a simple trigger on the handle, and hassle free with no charging and no cable getting tangled up as you clean. Sweapease is affordable and convenient- perfect for anyone with a tight budget. After cleaning your floors, you can simply throw away the bottom cotton pad and empty out the debris storage inside when you see fit. And with its collapsible handle, the SweapEase will take up not much space at all when storing.
