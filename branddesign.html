Daydream Overview

Day dream is a short animated film visualizing the contrast between a bright and whimsical fantasy world with the everyday environment of a metropolis winter. The latter is gloomy and somber, whereas the Day dream landscape is filled with every- thing from beams of colorful light to animals of non conven- tional scale and placement.
The video follows the everyday life of a college student in New York City who fantaszies about a more cheerful and exciting world. Bunnies hop on top of skyscrapers and fish guide her through crosswalks, livening her normally somber routine.
